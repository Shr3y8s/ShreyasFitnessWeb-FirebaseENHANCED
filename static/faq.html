Getting Started

How do I get started with training?

Don't worry - getting started doesn't have to be overwhelming! I've designed a simple process to make your fitness journey as smooth as possible.

The initial step is scheduling a complimentary 15-minute consultation where I'll answer your basic questions about my services, pricing options, and determine if we're a good fit to work together.

What to Expect in Your Free 15-Minute Consultation:
  • Brief Introduction: Learn about my training philosophy and approach
  • Service Overview: Get answers about my various training options
  • Pricing Questions: Discuss rates and package options
  • Basic Questions: Address any initial concerns or questions you may have

After you've booked a training package or membership, we'll schedule a comprehensive assessment session where we'll conduct a detailed health history review, set specific goals, and create your personalized fitness program.

This two-step process ensures you get all your questions answered before making a commitment, and then receive a fully personalized plan once you're ready to begin.

Ready to get started? Schedule your free consultation today!

Do I need to be fit already to start training?

No fitness experience required! I specialize in working with people at all fitness levels, including complete beginners.

My programs are designed to meet you exactly where you are and help you progress at an appropriate pace for your current fitness level. In fact, many of my clients have never set foot in a gym before working with me.

Every program starts with foundational movements and gradually builds in complexity as your strength, confidence, and skill level increase. You'll never be asked to do anything you're not prepared for.

What happens during the comprehensive assessment?

After you've signed up for a training package or membership, we'll schedule a comprehensive assessment session. This session lasts approximately 60 minutes and includes a detailed discussion about your health history, fitness background, specific goals, and lifestyle. We'll also conduct basic movement assessments to identify any limitations or imbalances. This information helps me create your personalized fitness plan that we'll implement in our training sessions.

What should I wear and bring to my training sessions?

For training sessions, wear comfortable athletic clothing and supportive shoes appropriate for your workout. Bring a water bottle, a small towel, and any specific equipment we've discussed (if applicable for home training). For gym sessions, a lock for the locker room may be useful. If you have a fitness tracker or heart rate monitor, feel free to bring it along. Most importantly, come with a positive attitude and be ready to work hard!

Training Information

Where do in-person sessions take place?

I offer training at partnered gyms throughout the Seattle area, as well as home training for clients who have basic equipment. The exact location will be determined during our consultation based on your preferences and proximity.

What equipment do I need for online coaching?

Equipment requirements for online coaching vary based on your goals and what you have access to. I can design programs using minimal or no equipment, or for fully-equipped home or commercial gyms. During our consultation, we'll discuss what you have available and create a plan accordingly.

How often will we meet for training?

The frequency of sessions depends on your goals, availability, and budget. Most clients see best results with 2-3 sessions per week, but I offer flexible scheduling options ranging from once weekly to daily coaching. We'll determine the optimal frequency during our consultation.

Services and Specialties

Do you offer nutrition planning?

Yes! Nutrition is a crucial part of achieving your fitness goals, and I provide practical nutrition guidance that works with your lifestyle and training program.
What I help you with:
  • Sustainable Eating Strategies: Practical approaches to healthy eating that fit your schedule and preferences
  • Meal Timing & Pre/Post Workout Nutrition: Optimize your energy and recovery through smart food timing
  • Portion Guidance & Food Choices: Learn to make better decisions without obsessive tracking
  • Supplement Recommendations: Evidence-based advice on what supplements might benefit your goals
  • Habit Building: Create lasting changes rather than short-term diet fixes
My approach focuses on building sustainable habits rather than restrictive diets. We'll work together to find nutrition strategies that support your training and fit your real life.
Important Disclaimer: I am not a registered dietician or nutritionist. While I provide general nutrition guidance based on fitness best practices, I refer clients to qualified nutrition professionals for medical conditions, eating disorders, or specialized dietary needs.

Do you work with clients who have injuries or medical conditions?

Yes, I work with clients who have various injuries and medical conditions, as long as they've been cleared for exercise by their healthcare provider. I collaborate with physical therapists and other healthcare professionals to ensure training programs are safe and effective. Please discuss any health concerns during our initial consultation.

Policies and Logistics

What is your cancellation policy?

I require 24 hours notice for session cancellations. Sessions cancelled with less than 24 hours notice may be subject to a cancellation fee. I understand that emergencies happen, so exceptions can be made on a case-by-case basis.

How are payments handled?

I accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards and digital payment platforms. Payments are typically collected at the beginning of each training package or on a monthly basis for ongoing clients. Detailed payment information will be provided during our initial consultation.

What kind of results can I expect?

Beyond physical changes, clients typically experience improved energy levels, better sleep quality, enhanced mood, increased strength and endurance, better posture, reduced pain, and greater confidence. The specific results depend on your goals—whether that's weight loss, muscle gain, improved athletic performance, or better overall health. I design programs to deliver comprehensive improvements in both how you look and how you feel.

Do you offer guarantees?

While I can't guarantee specific results due to the many individual factors involved in fitness transformations, I do guarantee my commitment to providing evidence-based programming, attentive coaching, and consistent support. I offer a satisfaction guarantee for new clients: if after your first two weeks you don't feel the service is a good fit, I'll refund your initial payment. The best results come from a collaborative approach where we both bring our full effort to the process.

What happens if I don't see progress?

If you're not seeing expected progress, we'll first review your adherence to the program, including workout consistency, nutrition, sleep, and recovery practices. Then, I'll adjust your program as needed—changing exercises, modifying training variables, or addressing lifestyle factors that may be hindering your progress. Plateaus are normal, and overcoming them often leads to the best long-term results. My job is to help identify what's holding you back and find effective solutions.
